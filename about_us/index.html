About Us

  1. Home

We are a top tier destination management company , we offer custom tailored Apes and Safaris Tours in East Africa

Then in 2000 when Joseph Birori had an idea to create a tour management company with one unescorted purpose to provide any traveler bored with mass tourism with exclusively unique and life changing experience tours, he named his idea Africa Apes Safaris LTD

18 years later with more than 200,000 Apes and safari Tours under our belt in Rwanda, Uganda, DRC, Kenya or Tanzania and the idea continues to grow and is firm strong it has ever been.
