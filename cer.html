CORPORATE ENVIRONMENTAL RESPONSIBILITY

Rwanda environment is our prime worry and we equally understand the need of our participation in community-based programs and projects. We safeguard the environment by creating a long-lasting positive impact on the communities where we do business. Our business appreciates that we exist and continue to prosper if we positively give back to the environment in which we operate. To this end we are committed to operate our business while ensuring to the best of our knowledge, our actions do not endanger the health or lives of people we work with and the environment we work in including plants and animals.

Africa Apes Safaris

Africa Apes Safaris is a tourism company based in vicinity of Volcanoes National Park (VNP). It operates in the whole country but it has head office in Rusizi District located in the Western Province/Rwanda.
